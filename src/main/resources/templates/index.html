Welcome in Bonn:

We would like to show you in our internet page the beauty and the manifoldness of Bonn, the former capital of Germany. It still is called "Bundesstadt Bonn", because there is still the seat of government and part of the German administration in Bonn.

But Bonn is not only famous for that. You can also find the headquarter of Deutsche Post and Deutsche Telekom.

For those who like culture, Bonn is also know for his famous composer and pianist, Ludwig van Beethoven, who is considered to be one of the greatest composers. He was born in Bonn and you can visit the house (Bethoven-Haus), which is a museum today with additional cultural activities around.

Sightseeing in Bonn

Interesting Buildings

You can find many intesting buildings in Bonn. Take the Post tower or the Bonn market square with the Old Town Hall or even the Bethoven Museeum.

Castles

Find out more about the Castle of Drachenburg, the Poppelsdorfer Schloss, close to the city center or the castle Augustusburg in Brühl, which you should visit if you like the castle of Neuschwanstein in Bavaria.

Surroundings

Worth visiting is of course the Cologne Cathedral, which is a known monument with Gothic architecture, which was started to build in 1248. We recommend the Phantasialand, for families, which is a theme park between Bonn and Cologne. Don't miss the Siebengebirge and the Eifel National Park.
